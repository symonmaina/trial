THIRU SCHOOL

Thiru secondary is a mixed day secondary school located in laikipia county, Laikipia west constituency. The principle is Madam Mercy Ochieng and the deputy is Mr Kariuki. Thiru secondary school or as they call themselves Thiru school is one of the best day school in the county and the country at large.


Thiru secondary school performance

Thiru school has alwys been the best day school in the county.It being a sub county school it competes with exra county schools and nation schools. In the year 2021 it took about 38 students to the university with one of the students having an A-. In 2022 it took 36 students to the university with the top 5 students having a B+.
